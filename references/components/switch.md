Switch is a binary toggle for settings that take effect immediately, like enabling a feature or turning on notifications. It accepts the following props: on (boolean, default false), state ("default" or "disabled", defaulting to "default"), inverted (boolean, default false), size ("small" or "medium", defaulting to "medium"), and label (string). The switch has two states: default and disabled. It comes in two sizes: small and medium. Its anatomy consists of a Root laid out horizontally with 8px gap and center alignment, a Left Label (always hidden), a Track (pill-shaped), a Knob (circle inside the track), and a Right Label. At medium size the track is 42x24 with 20px corner radius, and the knob is 16x16 in both on and off positions. At small size the track is 28x16 with 20px corner radius, and the knob is 12x12 when on or 10x10 when off. Typography uses Open Sans at weight 400 (Regular) for the label, with 14px/22px at medium size and 12px/16px at small size.
